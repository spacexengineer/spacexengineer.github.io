About Me

I am a full stack software developer in the Bay Area with experience in JavaScript, Python and C. I have some experience with relational and non-relational databases as well as basic knowledge when it comes to configuring Linux servers.

During my undergrad at Kettering University in Michigan, I had the opportunity to work in the automotive industry and kicked off my journey as a developer when I worked on firmware at Tesla Motors. Inspired by what I accomplished at Tesla, I took a shot at starting my own biotech algae company in Ireland with seed funding from RebelBio, but failed to take our company to the next stage.

I have returned to the Bay Area to build a strong foundation in software development from a first principle approach. Through educational resources, friends, and relationships that I have made in my network, I am looking forward to taking the next step in joining a team of developers and contribute to new, innovative technology.
